Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about our salon services and booking.

Do I need an advance appointment? +

While walk-ins are always welcome, we highly recommend booking an advance appointment to avoid wait times, especially on weekends and peak festival/wedding seasons.

What bridal packages do you offer? +

We offer customized Pre-Bridal grooming (facials, body wax, hair spa, manicure/pedicure) as well as Wedding Day HD & Airbrush Makeup packages. Contact us for trial sessions!

Do you provide home beauty services? +

Yes, home services are available for special bridal group packages and party makeup bookings within Hyderabad. Prior booking is required.

Which beauty brands do you use? +

We use premium, dermatologically tested brands such as MAC, Kryolan, L'Oréal Professional, Cheryl's, O3+, and Lotus Herbals for skin and hair treatments.
